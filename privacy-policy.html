The personal information we collect depends on which NexAgent AI product or service is in use. The following table sets out the data collected per product line:

Product Data Collected

3. Purpose of Processing

We process personal information solely for the following purposes:

  • To operate and deliver whichever NexAgent AI product(s) the client has subscribed to
  • To respond automatically to customer enquiries on behalf of our clients (WhatsApp AI Agent)
  • To generate, store, and surface job cards, quotes, leads, invoices, stock records, payroll records, and operational data for our clients (NexAgent OS modules)
  • To deliver WhatsApp notifications to clients and their customers (job completion alerts, quote confirmations, lead notifications)
  • To maintain service performance, diagnose issues, and improve the system

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal information on the following lawful bases under POPIA:

  • Legitimate interest: delivering contracted services to our business clients
  • Contract performance: processing necessary to fulfil our service agreement with clients
  • Consent: where end users interact with a NexAgent AI-powered WhatsApp number or application, they do so voluntarily

Note: Our clients (businesses that use the Service) are the "responsible party" in respect of their end-customers' data. NexAgent AI acts solely as an "operator" under POPIA and processes data only on behalf of and under the instruction of each client.
