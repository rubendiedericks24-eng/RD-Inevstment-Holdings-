1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how RD Investment Holdings (Pty) Ltd, trading as NexAgent AI ("we", "us", "our"), collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information in the course of providing our WhatsApp AI agent service ("Lexi"). We are committed to complying with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 ("POPIA") and all applicable South African data protection legislation.

2. What Personal Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • WhatsApp messages sent to Lexi, including the sender's WhatsApp number
  • Business data provided by our clients (listings, inventory, service descriptions)
  • Contact details of client businesses (name, email address, physical address)
  • Enquiry and booking data generated through interactions with Lexi

3. Purpose of Processing

We process personal information solely for the following purposes:

  • To operate and deliver the Lexi WhatsApp AI agent service to our clients
  • To respond automatically to customer enquiries on behalf of our clients
  • To generate lead notifications and booking confirmations for our clients
  • To maintain service performance, diagnose issues, and improve the system

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal information on the following lawful bases under POPIA:

  • Legitimate interest: delivering contracted services to our business clients
  • Contract performance: processing necessary to fulfil our service agreement with clients
  • Consent: where end users interact with a Lexi-powered WhatsApp number, they do so voluntarily

Note: Our clients (businesses that use Lexi) are the "responsible party" in respect of their end-customers' data. NexAgent AI acts solely as an "operator" under POPIA and processes data only on behalf of and under the instruction of each client.

5. Third-Party Processors

In delivering our service, we share data with the following third-party processors. All processors are bound by appropriate data processing terms.

6. Data Retention

Conversation data (WhatsApp message history stored for Lexi's contextual memory) is retained for a maximum of 6 months from the date of the relevant interaction, after which it is permanently deleted. Client business data is retained for the duration of the service agreement and deleted within 30 days of contract termination.

7. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject, you have the following rights under POPIA:

  • Access: request confirmation of whether we hold your personal information and a copy thereof
  • Correction: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Deletion: request deletion of your personal information where we no longer have lawful grounds to process it
  • Objection: object to the processing of your personal information
  • Complaints: lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (see below)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: rubendiedericks24@gmail.com

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information against unauthorised access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. Access to client data is restricted to authorised personnel only. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

9. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies. No personal information is collected through the rdinvestmentholdings.co.za website.
